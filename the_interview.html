It was a hefty sum they were going to pay. The interviewer had said that he would have to work seventy hours a week. Rahul however, didn’t take it as word. He thought the sum is hefty, he could work around something like sixty hours but for the money he had said yes. “ … from which me and my partner concluded that the cell structure in blood was changed during the evolution to accomodate the erect structure of human.” Rahul wanted to laugh when he said the word ‘erect’ but he didn’t allow that on the face. Oh I know all this, don’t be a smart ass. Why don’t I stop him. Mr Avasthi stopped him and said. “Good, I take as you a believer in the evolution rather than God … I will like”

Mr. Avasthi knew there was something that could be used against him. Something had said while giving introduction. Only if he had paid better attention. “I don’t believe in god. He doesn’t exist” He said.

“We believers in god” Rahul paused for a moment and added “believes that it is okay to not believe in god” . It was sarcastic and he could see on the face of interviewers but he meant to be sarcastic.

“That is arrogant, Mr Rahul” Kavya said who was dwelled in thoughts of her boyfriend. She was eager to finish off the interview early so, she can get back to Vihar’s apartment. “Okay. let’s move on to your personality, do you have any hobbies ?” Mr. Raina said.

“Well, in my lesuire time, oh I do enjoy being free. A popcorn tub in my lap” Although when Rahul said he was picturing the whole scenario in different setup. “And a television with Netflix subscription. Amazon is for cheap people.”

“Do you not know that all the movies on Netflix are on Amazon servers” Mr. Raina said. All the interviewer by now had understood that the person was not interested in taking the job. However, It was fun and very interesting than the previous candidate who had his shirt soaked with the sweat.

This was one of the swag moment, he imagined a flying gogles and a black cap with something written on it settled on his face. He didn’t like golden chains.

Kavya was sitting there restless, she knew that they were not going to select Rahul for the job. She realized however, he himself didn’t want the job. Her peers (She rarely called them peers - they were bunch of douchebags) were making fool of themselves. Kavya wouldn’t bear this long. However, she was the junior in the Hiring department. Who made them the boss she said to herself. “Cut to the chase, you are not here for the Job.” She said at last.

“And you are not exactly here, for hiring me or anyone else who is waiting outside, getting nervous and sweating heavily. So you better cut to the chase.” Rahul said.

“Others is no buisness of yours” Mr Raina said with anger. “We will do what we have to do”

Mr Avasthi turned his chair to his left and stared at Kavya. He didn’t speak but it was this - Two months you had. I have twenty years of experince. Shut the fuck up.

“What the hell, I am leaving.” Rahul said when he couldn’t bear the tension growing inside.

“Seat young man” Mr Avasthi said. The words and the look on his face didn’t match.

Rahul stopped and stood there beside the chair. “Okay” he mumbled to himself.

“You know who you are ?” Mr Avasthi said. “You are a hypocrite. You are having fun with yourself and telling us to shut the scene. I hope it’s not late for the company that offered you the job.” He grinned his broad smile.

Rahul lunged forward with his hand resting on the chair and smiled. “I can’t hope that for this company. It’s already late.” He walked out of the room. Bang Two !! He hoped there would be Avenger heros (except the jumping kid - he is too young to understand the concept of swag - better stick to the wall) bowing down to him in the lobby.
